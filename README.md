# Staying Housed in the City

**Staying Housed in the City** is an interactive fiction game exploring the challenges of finding affordable housing in a modern city. You play as a recent college graduate navigating rent prices, job offers, financial limitations, and the emotional rollercoaster of adulthood.

Built using Twine, this story-driven game invites players to make choices that shape their experienceâ€”balancing realism with empathy, humor, and social commentary.

## Features

- Dynamic branching narrative
- Real-world inspired housing dilemmas
- Multiple endings based on player choices
- Educational insights into tenant rights, affordability, and urban policy

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click through choices to navigate the story.
3. Your decisions influence both your outcome and the game's tone.

## Technologies Used

- [Twine](https://twinery.org/)
- HTML/CSS (single-page web app)
